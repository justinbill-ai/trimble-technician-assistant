Match your Siteworks project units before computing results.

Total station: Your CSV must include control points BL and BR (plus G, CT1, CT2, CL, BB). Use the offset field for rear of bracket to machine face if needed.

Manual tape: Enter the full distance between lift arms and the bracket offset below. BL and BR are not required in the CSV.

Total station: Your CSV must include CR (with CL, G, CT1, CT2, BB). Width is computed from coordinates.

Manual tape: Enter measured attachment width below. CR is optional in the CSV.

Survey data

Positioning the machine

Calibrating on a level pad is highly recommended. Siteworks Machine Guidance does not compensate for roll during measure-up. If the machine is not on level ground, preferably position it with pitch but no or very little roll.

Target layout
• CT1 & CT2 define forward direction — keep them parallel to centerline, on the same plane, and use the same target thickness.
• CL on a straight, unworn section of the cutting edge; CR on the opposite corner when measuring width.
• G is the attachment pivot pin.

Optional total-station points (see diagram)
• BL and BR — boom lift arms. If you choose Total station for centerline and shoot BL and BR, the calculator bisects that width to find center automatically.
• CR — opposite cutting-edge corner. If you choose Total station for width and shoot CR (with CL), the calculator computes cutting-edge width from coordinates.

Antenna bracket (BB)
• BB is the bottom bolt of the antenna bracket.
• After calculations, always confirm your centerline to the rear of the antenna bracket.
