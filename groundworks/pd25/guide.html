Guided pre measure-up workflow

Vermeer PD25 · Trimble Groundworks

Measure-up prerequisites
  • All shots must use zero rod height in Siteworks and be recorded as control points.
  • Plumb the mast in X and Y before shooting.
  • Slide the mast all the way left (toward the engine bay).
  • Retract the Y-carriage all the way to the rear of the machine.
  • Lower the mast foot as far down as possible.
  • Place the machine on a firm, level surface for measure-up.

Do not enter rod height on MB or H in Siteworks — enter post height in the measure-up calculator before you hit Calculate Results.

When survey is complete, open the measure-up calculator to upload your CSV and generate Groundworks values.
