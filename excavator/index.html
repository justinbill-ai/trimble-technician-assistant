Show Slider Reference

Direction guidance used by the assistant. Adjust one variable at a time, then recheck 2-3 passes.

  • Rake In Before/After Apex: increase to lift if cutting low; decrease if cutting high.
  • Rake Out Before/After Apex: increase to lift if cutting low; decrease if cutting high.
  • Boom Takeoff Speed: increase if not driving to grade early/cutting low; decrease if diving/oscillating at start.
  • Boom Raise Response + Speed: primary levers for Section A and entry to Section B.
  • Boom Lower Speed + Response: primary levers around apex and after-apex behavior.
  • Bucket Dump/Curl Speed + Response: flat profile shaping inward/outward passes.
  • Joystick Response Speed: secondary adjustment when oscillation persists after primary sliders.
Always cross-check final slider direction against the Commissioning Manual before handoff.
